DRAFT DOCUMENT DISCLAIMER

This medical certificate is generated as a DRAFT document only. It contains no diagnosis, no treatment recommendations, and no fitness determinations. The document serves as an administrative template to be completed by a reviewing clinician. It is not valid for any official purpose until properly reviewed, completed, and signed by a licensed healthcare provider. The reviewing clinician is solely responsible for all clinical content added to this document.

---

CERTIFICATE USAGE GUIDANCE

Medical certificates are commonly requested for attendance verification at medical appointments, documentation of rest periods recommended by healthcare providers, administrative records for workplace or educational institutions, and travel or activity clearance (requires clinical assessment). All certificates must clearly state their purpose and limitations. Draft certificates must never be presented as official documents.

---

CLINICIAN REVIEW REQUIREMENTS

Before signing, the reviewing clinician must verify patient identity and information, complete all clinical observations based on examination, determine appropriate recommendations based on clinical judgment, ensure compliance with institutional policies, remove or update any placeholder text, and add facility stamp and personal signature. The clinician assumes full responsibility for the final document content.
